Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How long does it take to get placed on playlists?
Most placements happen within 2-3 weeks of submission. Our curators take time to listen and consider each track carefully.
What genres do you work with?
We work with all major genres including pop, hip-hop, electronic, rock, R&B, and more. We have specialized curators for niche genres as well.
Can I submit multiple tracks?
Yes! We offer packages for multiple track submissions. We recommend focusing on your strongest single first.
How do you ensure playlist quality?
We vet all curators and only work with those who have real, engaged audiences and follow Spotify's guidelines.
Do I need to create an account to submit my song?
No, you can submit your song without creating an account, but we recommend signing up for a better experience and to track your submissions.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payments through our secure payment processor.
